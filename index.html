About


Hi, I'm Jenny your friendly neighbourhood barber. I have been in the business of cutting hair for 12 years. I have a large number of clients of diverse backgrounds and hair style needs. I am an avid marathon runner, a member of the 50 States Marathon Club, and completed all 50 states in January 2016. It is my passion to help others to look their best and feel confident about themselves when they walk out my door by giving them a great haircut. I look forward to meeting you at Jenny's Barber Shop and transforming your look to help you achieve great success.
