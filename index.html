About


Hi, I'm Jenny your friendly neighborhood barber! I have been in the business of cutting hair for 15 years. I have a large number of clients of diverse backgrounds and hair style needs, so I know what I’m doing.

Not only am I a licensed barber, but I also am an avid marathon runner and member of the 50 States Marathon Club of which I’ve completed running all 50 states back in January 2016. When you come to see me, you can check out some of the awards on the wall I’ve earned from many marathons I’ve ran throughout the U.S.

It is my passion to help others to look their best and feel confident about themselves when they walk out my door by giving them a great haircut. I look forward to meeting you at Jenny's Barber Shop, located inside iSalon on Louetta Road and transforming your look to help you achieve great success. I can’t wait to make you look your best!

Call me at (281) 455-4782 to set up your next appointment with me.
